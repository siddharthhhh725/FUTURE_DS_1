# Superstore Sales & Profit Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes Superstore sales data using Power BI to identify key business trends, profitability drivers, and growth opportunities. The dashboard provides insights into sales performance, profit distribution, customer segments, regional performance, and discount impact.

---

## Objective

The objective of this project is to:

- Analyze sales and profit performance.
- Identify top-performing categories and regions.
- Evaluate the impact of discounts on profitability.
- Discover growth opportunities for the business.
- Create a professional and interactive Power BI dashboard.

---

## Key Insights

### 1. Technology is the most profitable category.
Technology products generate the highest overall profit and contribute significantly to revenue.

### 2. Furniture category underperforms.
Furniture, especially Tables, records substantial losses despite strong sales volume.

### 3. West region leads performance.
The West region contributes the highest sales and profit among all regions.

### 4. Excessive discounts reduce profitability.
Higher discount levels significantly decrease profit margins.

### 5. Consumer segment drives most sales.
The Consumer segment remains the largest contributor to total revenue.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Increase focus on high-profit Technology products.
- Reduce discounts on low-margin products.
- Review pricing strategy for Furniture products.
- Expand marketing efforts in high-performing regions.
- Improve inventory planning for top-selling products.
