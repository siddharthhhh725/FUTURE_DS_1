Superstore Sales & Profit Analysis

Business Intelligence Dashboard using Power BI

Project Overview

This project analyzes Superstore sales performance, profitability, customer segments and regional trends using Power BI.

Business Problem

Identify profitable products, understand regional performance and determine how discounts impact profit.

Key Insights

  • Technology is the most profitable category.
  • Furniture contributes low profit despite strong sales.
  • West region generates highest revenue.
  • High discounts reduce profitability.
  • Consumer segment drives most sales.

Business Recommendations

  • Increase Technology product promotions.
  • Reduce excessive discounts.
  • Improve Furniture pricing strategy.
  • Expand successful regional strategies.

Tools Used

Power BI | Excel | DAX
